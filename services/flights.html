Flight Assistance

Planning your trip to Albania starts with finding the right flight. At Pompea Tours, we want to make that process as smooth as possible.

We Don't Book Flights – But We'll Help You Find Them!

Planning your trip to Albania is an exciting first step, and to make the process even smoother, we're happy to assist you in finding the perfect flights. While we don't directly book tickets, we can provide you with helpful resources and links to reputable booking platforms, ensuring you have access to the best available options for your travel dates and budget. This complimentary service is designed to simplify your travel arrangements, allowing you to focus on what truly matters: discovering the wonders of Albania.

Send Us Your Info

To help you efficiently, email us the following:

  • Departure city
  • Preferred travel dates
  • Arrival airport (usually Tirana or Corfu)
  • Number of travelers

Email us at ec5745@rit.edu and we’ll reply with the most convenient flight search suggestions or links.

We’re here to make your arrival in Albania as smooth and stress-free as possible!
