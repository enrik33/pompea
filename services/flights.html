Flight Assistance

Planning your trip to Albania starts with finding the right flight. At Pompea Tours, we want to make that process as smooth as possible.

We Don't Book Flights – But We'll Help You Find Them!

While we do not directly book airline tickets, we offer free assistance in helping you search for and compare flights that best suit your travel plans and budget. Simply send us your travel details, and we’ll point you toward the best options.

Send Us Your Info

To help you efficiently, email us the following:

  • Departure city
  • Preferred travel dates
  • Arrival airport (usually Tirana or Corfu)
  • Number of travelers

Email us at ec5745@rit.edu and we’ll reply with the most convenient flight search suggestions or links.

Flight Search Resources

You can also explore flights yourself using these platforms:

  • Google Flights
  • Skyscanner
  • Kayak
