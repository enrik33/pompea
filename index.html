About Pompea Tours & Travel

At Pompea Tour & Travel, we craft unforgettable private tours designed to showcase the very best of Albania. Our experienced, local guides, passionate about sharing their insider knowledge, lead you on enriching journeys through the country's hidden gems and iconic landmarks. Whether you seek thrilling adventures, cultural immersion, or tranquil relaxation, we offer tailored experiences that create lasting memories. Our mission is to provide seamless and enriching journeys that connect you with the authentic spirit of Albania.

  • Tailored Experiences: From seaside getaways to mountain treks — every tour is shaped around your travel style and interests.
  • Insider Expertise: Our guides are locals who love what they do and share real stories, not rehearsed scripts.
  • Seamless Travel: We handle the logistics so you can enjoy the experience — with trusted drivers, curated stops, and stress-free planning.
  • Authentic Connection: More than sightseeing — we help you connect with Albania’s heart, culture, and traditions.

Seaside Escapes

Experience the beauty of the Albanian coast! From Ksamil's turquoise waters to the historic Porto Palermo Castle, enjoy relaxing and exploring picturesque seaside towns.

History & Tradition

Discover Albania's rich history and traditions. Visit Berat, the "City of a Thousand Windows," and explore Gjirokastra's cobblestone streets and historic landmarks.

Transport Services

Travel comfortably in our spacious van, driven by an experienced driver. Perfect for small group tours or private journeys.
