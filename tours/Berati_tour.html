Berati Tour: Castle & UNESCO Old Town

Explore the "City of a Thousand Windows" and discover its rich history and stunning landscapes.

About the Tour

Your journey begins at 8 a.m. with a hotel pickup, starting a scenic 3-hour drive towards the historic city of Berat.

On the way, we will stop at Uji i Ftohte, a beautiful riverside spot where you can enjoy a coffee and soak in the natural surroundings.

Upon arrival in Berat, we kick things off with a delicious traditional lunch to fuel up for the day.

After lunch, we begin a guided tour of Berat's UNESCO-listed Old Town, where you'll explore the famous Berat Castle and its well-preserved churches, mosques, and historic houses.

As the sun sets, we start our comfortable journey back to Saranda, ending the tour with incredible memories of Berat's stunning architecture and heritage.

Itinerary

  • Stop 1: Uji i Ftohte - Enjoy a refreshing riverside stop.
  • Stop 2: Traditional Lunch - Savor delicious local flavors.
  • Stop 3: Berat Old Town - Explore the UNESCO World Heritage site.
  • Stop 4: Berat Castle - Walk through history at the hilltop fortress.
