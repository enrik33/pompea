Permeti Tour: Benje Thermal Baths & Uji i Ftohte

Relax and explore the beautiful natural landscapes of Permet, featuring soothing thermal baths and breathtaking scenery.

About the Tour

Your adventure begins at 8 a.m. with a hotel pickup, setting off on a scenic drive towards Uji i Ftohte, a refreshing stop by the river where you can enjoy a coffee and fill up your bottles with crisp natural spring water.

Upon arriving in Permet, we head straight to the Benje Thermal Baths, famous for their naturally warm and healing waters. Take time to soak and unwind in this incredible setting.

After your relaxing experience, we move to Permet town, where you'll have free time to enjoy a traditional late lunch and explore the charming streets.

Finally, we begin our comfortable journey back to Saranda, giving you time to reflect on a day filled with relaxation and stunning landscapes.

Itinerary

  • Stop 1: Uji i Ftohte - Enjoy the fresh mountain water and a short break.
  • Stop 2: Benje Thermal Baths - Soak in the healing warm waters.
  • Stop 3: Permet Town - Have a late lunch and explore the town.
