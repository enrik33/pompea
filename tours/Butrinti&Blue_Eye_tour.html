Basic Tour: Butrinti & Blue Eye

Discover ancient history and breathtaking nature in this half-day adventure to Butrint and the Blue Eye Spring.

About the Tour

This tour begins with a convenient hotel pick-up and a visit to the ancient city of Butrint, a UNESCO World Heritage site. Our expert guide will lead you through its fascinating ruins, revealing layers of history from its Greek origins to its Venetian rule.

From there, we journey to the breathtaking Blue Eye Spring, a natural wonder of vibrant turquoise waters. Witness the mesmerizing spectacle of crystal-clear water bubbling from the depths of the earth, creating a truly enchanting scene.

Lunch is available at a nearby restaurant (at your own expense), offering a chance to savor local cuisine amidst stunning natural surroundings.

Itinerary

  • Stop 1: Butrint - Explore the ancient ruins with a guided tour.
  • Stop 2: Blue Eye Spring - Experience one of Albania's most beautiful natural phenomena.
