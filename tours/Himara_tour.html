Himara Tour: Porto Palermo Castle & Beach

Experience the perfect mix of history and relaxation with breathtaking coastal views.

About the Tour

Your journey begins with a scenic drive along the stunning Albanian Riviera, heading towards Porto Palermo Castle, a fortress built by Ali Pasha of Tepelena.

After exploring the historic castle, we continue to the beautiful beaches of Himara, where you can relax, swim, or explore the charming coastal town.

A delicious seaside lunch will be arranged at a local restaurant, allowing you to enjoy fresh seafood and traditional dishes.

After soaking up the sun and enjoying the crystal-clear waters, we begin our return to Saranda with unforgettable memories.

Itinerary

  • Stop 1: Porto Palermo Castle - Explore the historic fortress.
  • Stop 2: Himara Beaches - Enjoy free time for swimming and relaxation.
  • Stop 3: Seaside Lunch - Taste fresh local seafood.
