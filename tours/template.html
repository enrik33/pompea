Basic Tour: Butrint & Blue Eye

Explore the ancient city of Butrint and the stunning Blue Eye spring on this unforgettable journey.

About the Tour

Our driver will pick you up from your hotel, ready to kick off an exciting day of adventure as we head to your first stop: the enchanting ancient city of Butrint. Accompanied by our knowledgeable and passionate tour guides, you’ll dive into the rich history and stories that make this place so special.

Our journey continues to Lekuresi Castle, where you’ll get to absorb the captivating history of this historic landmark that overlooks the city. Don’t forget to capture those Instagram-worthy shots of the stunning views!

Next, your tour continues to the stunning Blue Eye, a mesmerizing natural spring with vibrant turquoise waters. You'll be captivated by the sight of crystal-clear waters bubbling up from the depths, creating a unique and enchanting spectacle. This serene spot is the perfect place for you to relax and take in the beauty of nature.

If you'd like to sit down for lunch at the restaurant, just let the driver know. It's a great spot to grab a bite, though keep in mind that lunch isn't included in the tour. Enjoy your meal!

Itinerary

  • Stop 1: Butrint - Explore the ancient ruins with a guided tour.
  • Stop 2: Lekuresi Castle - Enjoy panoramic views over Saranda.
  • Stop 3: Blue Eye Spring - Witness the breathtaking beauty of the natural spring.
