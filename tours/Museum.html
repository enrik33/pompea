Experience the perfect blend of history, culture, and scenic views in this enriching Saranda-focused tour.

About the Tour

Your day begins with a hotel pick-up and a journey to the ancient city of Butrint. Our expert guide will lead you through this UNESCO World Heritage site, uncovering layers of history from its Greek origins to its Venetian rule. Explore the impressive theater, the Lion Gate, and the captivating museum within the site.

After exploring Butrint, we will enjoy a traditional Albanian lunch at a local restaurant in Karahaxh. The restaurant is known for its charming atmosphere and views of the surrounding countryside.

We'll then ascend to Lekuresi Castle, offering stunning panoramic views of Saranda's Bay and the surrounding hills. Learn about the castle's role in local history and capture memorable photos.

Our next stop is the Saranda Museum of Traditions, where you'll delve into the region's rich cultural heritage. Discover traditional costumes, artifacts, and exhibits showcasing local customs and crafts.

Finally, we visit the historic city basilica, where our guide will highlight its unique architectural features and historical significance.

Itinerary

  • Stop 1: Butrint - Guided exploration of ancient ruins.
  • Stop 2: Karahaxh - Enjoy traditional Albanian lunch.
  • Stop 3: Lekuresi Castle - Panoramic views and local legends.
  • Stop 4: Museum of Traditions - Cultural exhibits and crafts.
  • Stop 5: Basilica - Historical insights and architecture.
