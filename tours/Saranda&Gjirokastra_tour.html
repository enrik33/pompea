Two Cities Tour: Saranda & Gjirokastra

Explore the history and charm of two of Albania's most beautiful cities in one unforgettable day.

About the Tour

Your journey begins with a hotel pickup as we set off for a historical adventure. Our first stop is the Basilica in Saranda, where our guide will share fascinating insights into its rich past.

Next, we head to the ancient city of Butrint, a UNESCO World Heritage site. Here, you'll dive into centuries of history and explore the well-preserved ruins with an expert guide.

After exploring Butrint, we take a break at Karahaxh for a delicious lunch. Keep an eye out for ducks and swans around the area!

The adventure continues in Gjirokastra, known as the "City of Stone." You'll explore the grand Gjirokastra Castle, offering breathtaking views and an insight into Albania's military history. Strolling through the charming cobbled streets of the old town will transport you back in time.

After an enriching day, we return to Saranda, allowing you to reflect on your unforgettable experience.

Itinerary

  • Stop 1: Basilica in Saranda - Historical insights.
  • Stop 2: Butrint - Explore the ancient ruins.
  • Stop 3: Karahaxh - Enjoy a relaxing lunch.
  • Stop 4: Gjirokastra - Visit the castle and old town.
